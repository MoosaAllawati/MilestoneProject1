Moosa Al Lawati is a London-based civil engineer, and classical guitarist from Muscat, the Sultanate of Oman. Self-taught from the age of twenty, Moosa grew to perform in Flamenco and Arabesque styles of music. His independent album "Fireworks in Andalusia" will release in June 2024 across all music platforms. He aspires to support guitar learners of all ages, and collaborate with emerging artists from the Arab world and beyond. If you're interested in collaboration for an event, learning how to play, or general inquiries, please get in touch.
