Moosa Al Lawati is a London-based civil engineer, and classical guitarist from Muscat, the Sultanate of Oman. Self-taught from the age of twenty, Moosa grew to perform in Flamenco and Arabesque flavours of music with his odd time-signatures, rhythmic compositions, and fingerpicking styles.

His independent album "Fireworks in Andalusia" will release in June 2024 across all major music platforms. He aspires to support guitar learners of all ages, and collaborate with emerging bands or artists from the Arab world and beyond. If you're interested in holding an event, learning how to play, or general inquiries, get in touch.
